"The difference between ordinary and extraordinary is practice." -Vladimir Horowitz
Being a musician in the 21st century takes a lot of work. From private lessons to large-scale instruction, solo performers to orchestral musicians, and the practice room to the concert hall, making music involves preparation, improvement, and development as a person and performer. It's also a lot to manage - musicians and their mentors need to organize their time while effectively tackling the great musical problems laid before them.

Whether it's that one tricky measure, a new technique, or putting it all together as an ensemble, the work of a musician can be arduous. Yet our music can move hearts, open minds, impact communities, ignite discussions, and so much more.

Practirio's mission is to help musicians be the effective practicers and learners they can be. By managing our achievements and progress, collaborating with our peers and teachers, and informing our practical and musical decisions, Practirio enables today's students to become the next generation of music performers, educators, artists, and enthusiasts.
From the First Lesson to the Degree Recital Practirio is built for all levels of musician, from one's first lessons to advanced college undergraduate or graduate studies. Students and instructors can keep track of their lessons, highlight areas of success and improvement, and develop personalized plans to best develop musical potential. Take Practicing to the Next Level Had a rough week of practicing? We've been there. Sync your practicing tasks from your studios and ensembles and get to it! Practirio allows you to track your practice sessions, create and work through warm-up routines, divide and log your time spent on each task, and keep a practice journal, all while providing you analysis of your practicing to help you develop better practice habits, for your craft and for your health.


Control your Musical Life With Practirio, all of your musical organizations are in one place. Events, practice logs, lesson notes, and more integrate together to help you make music no matter how busy you may be. Musicians are always on the go, and so is Practirio. Use the app on your desktop or laptop computer, or download Practirio on your phone or tablet to use in the practice room. Manage Your Organizations With Ease From private studios, primary/secondary music programs, college studios and ensembles, and beyond, Practirio helps manage and connect musicians together. Create studios and ensembles, share rehearsal and event schedules, and manage your organization's inventory and digital resources, all in one convenient platform.
