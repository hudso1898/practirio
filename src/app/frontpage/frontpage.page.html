"The difference between ordinary and extraordinary is practice." -Vladimir Horowitz
Being a musician in the 21st century takes a lot of work. From private lessons to large-scale instruction, from solo performances to symphony orchestras, and from the practice room to the concert hall, making music involves preparation, improvement, and development as a person and performer. It's also a lot to manage - musicians and their instructors need to organize their time while effectively tackling the great musical problems laid before them.

Whether it's that one tricky measure, a new technique, or putting it all together as an ensemble, the work of a musician can be arduous. Yet our music can move hearts, open minds, impact communities, ignite discussions, and so much more.

Practirio's mission is to help musicians be the effective practicers and learners they can be. By managing our achivemenets and progress, connecting to our peers and teachers, and informing our practical and musical decisions, Practirio enables today's students to become the next generation of music performers, educators, artists, and appreciators.
